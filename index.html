About Sankalp

Being the oldest and Mother of all branches of engineering, Civil Engineering has a lot of opportunities and various dimensions to explore, all you need is a reliable platform. With this aim in mind, Sankalp, Civil Engineering Society has been established in the year 2019 by some of the B. Tech students of CE 2022 batch to encourage the students and glaze their abilities to be a confident engineer in the future and contribute positively in the development of our country. We provide a learning environment that encourages not only the academic success of civil engineering students but also focus on personal development and other extracurricular student activities.With the chain of activities going round the year, students get ample opportunity to flash, flourish and furnish their talents. Through this, we try to fill the gap in between practical and theoretical knowledge so that we will become ready for the industry work. We organize the annual fest of Civil Engineering Department 'SPANDAN' to provide the students to create, innovate and learn various aspects of civil engineering through competitions and events.
